Box Trailers

GFAB Trailers produces custom box trailers built to suit the specific requirements and criteria of our customers. Of late we have built box trailers for trades and construction business, sports teams, personal use, race cars and motocross bikes transportation

The main chassis of these trailers is built out of lightweight, high tensile, transport grade aluminium and the box can either be produced in 4mm ACM signage panel or 50mm poly panel.

There are various finishing options and odd on's available for these trailers and as each trailer is built to order it can customised to suit whatever you may need.
