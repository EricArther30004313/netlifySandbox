Alloy Fabrication

Although building quality aluminium trailers and boats is our main game, we definitely do not limit ourselves to just this type of work.

GFAB also has a great reputation for fabricating one off general alloy engineering requirements. Aluminium fire guards, commercial shop stands and racking, bunds, water tanks, fences and balustrades to name a few.

Your piece can be provided to you in raw alloy or powder coated by our neighbors BPC, who we work closely with on a day to day basis.
