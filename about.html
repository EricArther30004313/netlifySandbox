About GFAB

GFab Trailers is a locally owned aluminium fabrication company operating in the sunny Bay of Plenty. Here at GFab we specialise in the design and fabrication of custom alloy boats, alloy boat trailers and a wide range of high capacity general and specialist alloy trailers.

The owners of GFab, Glen Warner and Danny Sunkel, know all things boats and trailers. They pride themselves on working with each individual customer to ensure they receive the exact custom product they require to suit their specific needs.

All welding work is completed by Glen and his team. Glen has been welding boats, trailers and other general fabrication work for approximately 20 + years. So, you can guarantee that your alloy fabrication project is in good hands and will be built to a high standard.

As well as designing and building new boats and trailers, GFab also provides insurance boat and trailer repairs, modification work to existing boats and trailers, alloy trailers for a number of big boat brands, contract boat building and general fabrication work.
