Sportsdeck Trailers

Following on from a demand for light weight trailers by the side by side teams, GFAB have designed and fabricated a range of aluminium Sportsdeck trailers.

These trailers are built as a super light weight trailer, used for transporting sporting toys and general purpose transportation.

Our Sportsdeck trailers come either as a full model with built in toolbox, hardwood ply flooring and a single braked axle or a "lite" model.

There are a number of add on's available for these trailers, to ensure that your sporting equipment is transported adequately.
