Sports Trailers

GFab Sports Trailers are a popular option for a top of the range "all rounder" trailer, that will do a bit of everything for you.

They are heavy duty, light weight aluminium trailers and have a tare weight between 400kg - 500kg. All GFAB trailers are built using high tensile marine and transport grade alloy.

Optional extras available for our sports trailers -

  • Built in under deck ramp storage
  • Winch
  • Electric brakes
  • Canopy frame
  • Stock crate
  • Lift up tie down points
  • H frame racks
  • UHMWPE Skids
  • Slide out BBQ or custom storage options
