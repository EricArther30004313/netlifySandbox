Alloy Roofing and Builders Trailer

Due to demand from roofers and builders for lightweight trailers, we design and produce a specialist range of custom aluminium trailers that are purpose built for the roofing and building industry.

These trailers have built in drawbar toolboxes and have the option to include 2 metre pull out extensions. Other options that can be included are drop in racking or storage systems that are specific to the customers requirements.

Being aluminium, these trailers have a low tare weight of between 380kg - 650kg.

All our trailers are built using high tensile marine and transport grade alloy and are finished using a hardwood truck deck ply.
