Scissor Lift Trailers

GFAB aluminium scissor lift trailers are based off our popular sports trailer design. The design has been modified to house and transport both tyre and tracked scissor lifts.

The trailer in the picture is a heavy duty, high tensile alloy tandem 2500 scissor lift, rated to 3,500kg. With a tare weight of 550 - 600kg, this gives a transportable capacity of approx. 2,900kg!!

Optional extras available for our scissor lift trailers -

  • winch
  • canopy frame
  • stock crate
  • lift up tie down points (six are included)
  • slide out BBQ or custom storage options
